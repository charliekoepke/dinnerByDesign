Directions

  1. Measure out 1 and 1/2 cups of medium grain white rice.
  2. Rinse the rice thouroughly until the water is clear.
  3. Add 2 cups of water to the rice cooker.
  4. Allow the rice to soak fo 15 minutes then press cook on the rice cooker.
