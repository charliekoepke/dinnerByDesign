Guided Recipes

The goal of our recipes is to guide you through the cooking process in a way that minimizes mistakes. First, you can check off your ingredients and equipment as you gather them. Once you are ready to begin cooking, you can reference the interactive videos. Generally, each recipe is split into Prep and Cook videos. Once you start the guided recipe, it will loop through each step until you are ready to go on. You can move to the next step, or go back to the previous one using the arrows next to the video. As each step plays, you can also reference the written instructions below the video.
