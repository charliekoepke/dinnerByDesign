Crispy Skin Duck Breast

This cooking method leaves you with a duck breast with perfectly rendered crispy skin, as well as a beautiful medium rare throughout. Be sure to start with a cold pan and allow it to rest completely for best results.

Directions

Prep

  1. Gather tools and ingredients.
  2. Pat duck breast dry.
  3. Score skin with Chef's knife.
  4. Season with salt on both sides and pepper on meat side.

Cook

  1. Place skin side down in a cold pan on low-medium heat.
  2. Allow skin to render until the fat has rendered and is crispy.
  3. Flip over and baste with duck fat - allow to cook on meat side on medium heat for 90 seconds - adjust time for thicker duck breast.
  4. Allow duck to rest for 5-8 minutes.
  5. Cut duck into inch thick pieces.
